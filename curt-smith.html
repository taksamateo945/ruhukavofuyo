Curt Smith Biography

Curt Smith is best known as Musician, songwriter, producer who has an estimated Net Worth of $20 Million. English musician best known for forming the popular band Tears for Fears. He is known for the hits “Mad World” and “Everybody Wants to Rule the World.” He was born on June 24,1961, England. He also released four albums as a solo artist to varying degrees of success. He met band mate Roland Orzabal when they were teenagers, and they formed several bands before launching Tears for Fears in 1981. His hit song “Mad World” was famously covered by Gary Jules for the film Donnie Darko. He was married to Lynda Altman from 1982 to 1988 and married Frances Pennington in 1996.
