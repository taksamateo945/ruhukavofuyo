WHY ARE PEOPLE SEARCHING ‘HAS MADELEINE MCCANN BEEN FOUND’?

People have started searching whether Madeleine has been found after it emerged that clairvoyant Michael Schneider, 50, contacted the police with the coordinates of where he believes her body is located.

Schneider claimed her body is located just six miles from where she disappeared in Praia da Luz, Portugal, on May 3, 2007.

Using his own technique where he looks at a missing person’s photo and asks himself “upwards towards God whether this living being is alive or dead” before running his fingers and eyes over an atlas, Google Maps and Google Earth, Schneider has helped police locate missing people in a number of cases.

He was able to help police locate a missing woman, who was found hanging from a tree, within 36 hours.

He also found the location of murdered couple Peter Neumair, 63, and Laura Perselli, 68, after they vanished from their home in Bolzano, Italy, in January 2021.
