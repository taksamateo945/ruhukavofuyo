A young woman has revealed the two changes she made to transform her appearance.

Emily Brogan, an Australian living in London, showed a snap of what she once looked like and left fans in disbelief, with many saying she now looks like a ‘completely different person’.

Sharing before and after images with her 450,000 TikTok followers, Emily said she’s ‘grateful’ for two things that were key to her transformation: exercise and lip filler.

She also dyed her brown hair blonde and hasn’t looked back since. 

Emily started out walking more often and focused on a calorie deficit to lose weight, but now she focuses on building strength by lifting weights and running.
